HDFC | RBI Card Performance Dashboard

Business Analyst Micro-Experience Project with bluetick.ai

Project Overview

This project was built during a micro-experience program by bluetick.ai in collaboration with HDFC Bank. It simulates the work of a Business Analyst at HDFC, focusing on comparing debit and credit card performance against industry benchmarks. The final dashboard helped drive data-driven strategic planning and decision-making.
