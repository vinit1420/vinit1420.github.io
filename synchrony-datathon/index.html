Synchrony Datathon 2025

Credit Risk Prediction · Customer Segmentation · Personalized Line Increases

Overview

Our project for Synchrony’s 2025 Illini Datathon tackled three key problems:

  • 📈 Forecast Q4 2025 customer spending
  • 🧠 Segment accounts by credit risk and eligibility
  • 💳 Recommend personalized credit line increases

Tech Stack

  • 📊 Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, XGBoost, RandomForest)
  • 📈 Jupyter Notebook for experimentation and pipeline development
  • ⚖️ SMOTE for class imbalance handling
  • 📉 GradientBoostingRegressor & XGBoostClassifier for prediction & segmentation
  • 🧹 Data cleaning, merging, and feature engineering on multi-source structured data

Results

  • 📈 Q4 Spend Prediction Accuracy: R² score of 0.9985 with RMSE of 64.046
  • 🧠 Customer Segmentation: Overall accuracy of 88% using XGBoost + SMOTE
  • 💳 Credit Line Recommendations: Mean error of just $3.38 per customer using RandomForestRegressor
  • 🔍 Identified 2,788 high-risk accounts with 100% precision and no false positives
  • 🚀 Segmentation correctly identified 70 eligible-but-risky accounts out of a rare class
